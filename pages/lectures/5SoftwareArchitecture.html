What is Software Architecture?

"The fundamental organization of a software system embodied in its components, their relationships to each other and to the environment, and the principles guiding its design and evolution."

- IEEE Definition

Think of it as the blueprint for your software system!

Choosing Database Patterns

Use Shared Database When:

  • Small team/application
  • Strong data consistency needed
  • Simple transactions
  • Limited scale requirements

Use Multiple Databases When:

  • Large, distributed teams
  • Microservices architecture
  • Need to scale independently
  • Different data requirements
